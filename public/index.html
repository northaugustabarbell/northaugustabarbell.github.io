North Augusta Barbell is the leading Olympic Weightlifting facility in the CSRA and is operating in conjunction with Crossfit North Augusta. The only one of its kind in the area, NAB is fully equipped with high quality competition kilogram bumper plates and barbells. The facility offers personal training, group training, and flexible open gym hours and is open to all athletes interested in Olympic Weightlifting.
